---
title: Revoke a message (delete for everyone)
description: >-
  Deletes the message identified by `mid` in the path **for everyone** in the
  chat — not just on your own device. Located by `chat` (and `sender`) in the
  body.


  Use the `sender` body field to revoke another participant's message (e.g. as a
  group admin); leave it empty to revoke your own message.


  This is a synchronous operation and returns **200** with a `SendResult`.
  WhatsApp enforces its own time/role limits on who may revoke what.


  ### Preconditions & errors

  - Requires the **`send`** capability.

  - **404 `not_found`** — the session or message does not exist or is not owned
  by your organization.
