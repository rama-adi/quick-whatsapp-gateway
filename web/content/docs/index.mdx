The gateway exposes one WhatsApp account as an organization-scoped REST API. You
pair a phone once, then send messages, read chats and contacts, manage groups,
and receive delivery and message events over webhooks.

## How requests are authenticated

Every call carries one Bearer token, either:

- a **user JWT** (minted by the dashboard, verified against the gateway's JWKS), or
- an **API key** (prefix `wa_`, for machines) — sent as `Authorization: Bearer …` or in the `x-api-key` header.

Sessions belong to an organization. A token only reaches the sessions its
organization owns.
